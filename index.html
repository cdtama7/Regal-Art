RAS: Regal Art Search

An app that provides information on art in real time!

Search for an Artist, title or even topic of a painting you'd like to see and click "behold!" Results on the left are paintings from the Harvard Art Museum's collection. Be sure to click on the painting to take a closer look. On the right you'll find online book resources pertaining to your search. To read up on a specific artist enter their name into the search bar and click on the links under the "books" column.
